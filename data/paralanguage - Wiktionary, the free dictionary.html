Etymology

[ edit ]

From para- +‎ language .

Noun

[ edit ]

paralanguage ( countable and uncountable , plural paralanguages )

  1. ( linguistics ) The non-verbal elements of speech , and to a limited extent of writing, used to modify meaning and convey emotion , such as pitch , volume , and intonation
