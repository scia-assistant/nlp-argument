Pronunciation

Verb

[ edit ]

raise the bar ( third-person singular simple present raises the bar , present participle raising the bar , simple past and past participle raised the bar )

  1. ( idiomatic ) To raise standards or expectations , especially by creating something to a higher standard.
    Synonym: lift the bar
    Antonym: lower the bar
    Acme's new technology will raise the bar for the entire industry.
