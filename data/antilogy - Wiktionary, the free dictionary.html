English

[ edit ]

Pronunciation

[ edit ]
  • IPA ( key ) : /ænˈtɪləd͡ʒi/

Noun

[ edit ]

antilogy ( plural antilogies )

  1. ( rhetoric ) A contradiction in related terms or ideas. Usually an inconsistency in syllogisms , of a person or group supposedly of one set of ideals .
